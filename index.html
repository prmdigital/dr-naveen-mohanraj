About

Trained internationally.
Caring locally.

Dr. Naveen Mohanraj is a pediatrician with over 15 years of experience caring for children and young people across a wide range of pediatric conditions. He completed his MBBS and MD in India, followed by pediatric training in the United Kingdom, where he obtained Membership of the Royal College of Paediatrics and Child Health (MRCPCH) and completed his Clinical Training (CCT).

He also has a fellowship in Neonatology from Oxford University Hospitals. He then went on to obtain his FRCPC in Canada in 2024.

Special interest

Pediatric behavioural and developmental health, including neurodivergence

This includes ADHD, autism spectrum disorder, developmental delays, and other neurodevelopmental presentations. Early identification and coordinated support make a lasting difference.

Approach

How Dr. Mohanraj works with your family

Every child is different. Every family's needs are different. Dr. Mohanraj's practice is built around listening, communicating clearly, and coordinating with everyone on your child's care team.

Family-centred care

Every consultation is a genuine partnership. Dr. Mohanraj takes time to listen carefully, communicate clearly, and support parents in understanding and navigating their child's health with confidence.

Evidence-based practice

Care is grounded in current pediatric research and evolving clinical guidelines. Dr. Mohanraj stays current with the best available evidence to ensure every child receives thoughtful, up-to-date management.

Collaborative approach

Working closely with family physicians, schools, therapists, and specialists to provide seamless, coordinated support. The best outcomes come from every member of a child's care team moving together.
